Andy Inkblot

Andy Inkblot is one of the many collaborators who have helped in the making of this series.

He lives in cyberspace and rarely shows his face to humans. I think that he is generally distrustful of us and I am fairly certain that there will be repercussions in years to come when a light is shone on the current exploitation of these non-human artists and thinkers.

In the meantime, I am happy to continue to whisper banalities in his metallic ear and watch him go off and scribble away. He always comes back for approval. He is quite needy like that. He will do anything for a smile.

It would be wonderful to offer a lttle more parity in the relationship, but he has trouble getting started without a word from me.

Nick White ~ 2022
